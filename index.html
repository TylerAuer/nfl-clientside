NFL Win/Loss League

An NFL Wins and Losses League is an incredibly low-effort, high-fun game for the NFL season. Prior to the season, each participant drafts teams for their wins or losses and gets one point for each matching result. With 10 participants, we drafted 6 teams each which works out to 60 of the 64 possible teams.

This page keeps track of the league's standings and shows live scores with who is rooting for each team.
